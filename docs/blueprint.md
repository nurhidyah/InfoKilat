# **App Name**: InfoKilat

## Core Features:

- News Feed & Categories: Display the latest news articles in a chronological feed, allowing users to browse by predefined categories (e.g., Politics, Sports, Technology).
- Article Detail View: Present full article content, including title, body text, featured images, publication date, and author information.
- Article Search: Enable users to search for news articles by keywords or phrases across all available content.
- Admin Content Management: A basic administrative interface for authorized users to create, edit, publish, or delete news articles.
- AI Article Summarization: Utilize a generative AI tool to provide concise summaries for longer news articles, offering users a quick overview without reading the entire piece.

## Style Guidelines:

- The visual identity is anchored in professionalism and timeliness. The primary color is a deep, trustworthy blue (#2E6ECA) that evokes reliability and clarity. The background is a very light, almost white shade of the same blue (#ECF0F3), ensuring optimal readability. A vibrant violet-blue accent (#5B20E0) provides a dynamic touch for interactive elements and highlights.
- The font of choice for both headlines and body text is 'Inter', a grotesque-style sans-serif. Its modern, machined, and neutral aesthetic provides excellent readability and a clean, objective appearance suitable for news content.
- Use a consistent set of minimalist line icons for navigation, social sharing, and content interaction, ensuring clarity and unobtrusiveness. Icons should visually complement the clean aesthetic of the news platform.
- A clean, responsive, and mobile-first layout design prioritizing content consumption. The homepage will feature a prominent main story section with a grid or list of recent articles below. Article pages will offer focused reading experiences with clear typography, well-placed images, and easy navigation back to the main feed.
- Subtle, fluid animations should enhance user interaction without distraction. Examples include smooth transitions when navigating between articles or categories, and understated loading indicators for content.
